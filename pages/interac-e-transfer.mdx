# Interac e-Transfer with PaySecure

Interac is a leading Canadian payment network that facilitates secure, real-time transactions between bank accounts. It offers multiple payment methods, including **Request Money**.

PaySecure’s **Interac e-Transfer** product suite enables merchants to initiate:

- **PayIns**: Consumers can utilize Interac e-Transfer to transfer funds from their bank account via their bank’s app or website.
- **Payouts**: Facilitate real-time disbursements directly to consumers' accounts.

## Interac Request Money

The **Interac Request Money** method allows users to send and receive money directly between bank accounts using email. It provides a fast, secure, and convenient alternative to traditional payment methods, accessible through most Canadian banks' online and mobile banking platforms.
